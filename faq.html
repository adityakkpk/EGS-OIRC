Frequently Asked Question

The ICMMCS will take place from November 11-13, 2025, in Muscat, Oman. The exact venue details will be shared closer to the event date.

The conference is open to researchers, scholars, professionals, and students in the fields of mathematics, management, and computer science. It is also an excellent platform for industry leaders and practitioners to present their work and engage with peers.

To register for the conference, visit the "Registration" section, fill in the required details, and submit the form. Early bird registration offers discounted rates, so be sure to register early!

Abstracts and full papers can be submitted via the "Call For Paperss" section on our website. Please follow the guidelines for submission, which include formatting requirements and important deadlines. The deadline for paper submission is 30th Aug 2025.

The conference will cover a wide range of topics within mathematics, management, and computer science.

Yes, selected papers and abstracts will be presented at the conference through oral sessions. Authors of accepted papers will be notified well in advance with detailed instructions for their presentations.

Yes, attendees who are not submitting papers can still participate in the conference by registering as regular participants. You will be able to attend sessions, network with other professionals, and gain insights from the keynote speakers.

Yes, there is a registration fee for participants. The fee includes access to all sessions, conference materials, refreshments, and participation in networking events. Early bird rates will be available, so be sure to register early to secure the best rates.

We offer special travel and accommodation packages for conference attendees. Information on recommended hotels, travel tips, and local attractions will be available on the conference website. Participants are also welcome to arrange their own travel and accommodation.

For any inquiries, you can contact the conference organizing committee via email at info@icmmcs.com or visit our "Contact Us" page on the website. We are happy to assist with any questions you may have.

Yes! The ICMMCS will provide several opportunities for networking, including coffee breaks, social events, and a dedicated networking session. This is an excellent chance to meet other researchers, professionals, and organizations in your field.

Yes, we offer sponsorship and exhibitor opportunities for companies and organizations looking to gain visibility and connect with attendees. For more details, please visit the "Sponsorship" page on our website or contact us directly.

Yes, all registered participants will receive a certificate of attendance after the conference concludes. This will be issued electronically and can be used for professional development purposes.

We are committed to provide the Intersection of Mathematical Insight, Management Excellence, and Technological Innovation.
