About ICMMSC 2025

Theme

"Bridging Disciplines, Inspiring Innovation" - ICMMSC provides a dynamic convergence of knowledge, research, and industry expertise across interconnected fields.

Opportunities

Present research and publish in ESCI and SCOPUS-indexed journals, showcasing ideas through poster presentations, and establish global partnerships.

Objectives of ICMMCS 2025

ICMMCS 2025 aims to inspire future innovations by providing a vibrant platform for thought leaders, industry experts, and young researchers to explore challenges and drive forward-thinking solutions.
