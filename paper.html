Call For Papers

Paper Submission Guidelines

Submission Format

Authors are invited to submit original, unpublished research work in the form of full papers (8-15 pages) in MS-Word/.PDF

Submission Portal

Submissions should be made through the official conference submission portal

https://ICMMCS.ORG/paper.html

Review Process

All submissions will undergo a rigorous peer review process by an expert panel. Papers will be evaluated based on originality, significance and quality.

Requirement

Accepted papers must be presented at the conference by at least one of the authors.
